## Modernized Federal Talent Recruitment and Acquisition

We are revolutionizing OPM's talent ecosystem by accelerating recruitment of skilled professionals, including young tech-savvy talent, while eliminating vulnerabilities to waste, fraud, and abuse through automated, secure verification and data standards.

- **Who this serves:** Aspiring federal employees, including young professionals, students, Veterans, and diverse job seekers; current federal workers seeking career growth; and agencies needing top talent to drive mission success.
- **What's changing:** Legacy paper-based and slow hiring processes replaced with efficient, digital workflows that enable paperless transfers and targeted recruitment.
- **The impact:** Slashing hiring timelines from months to weeks, preventing billions in improper payments and inefficiencies, boosting workforce diversity with a focus on young innovators, and enhancing overall federal performance.
